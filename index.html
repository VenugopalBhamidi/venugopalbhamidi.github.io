About Me

I am a second-year postgraduate researcher in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at IIT Gandhinagar. My research is centered on the intersection of Empathic Speech Synthesis (TTS) and Adversarial Robustness in Federated Learning.

As the Senator for M.Tech students and Branch Representative of AI, I advocate for student welfare and foster connections between academic research and industry opportunities. I also serve as a Teaching Assistant at IIT Gandhinagar, where I take pride in mentoring the next generation of AI practitioners.

My mission is to develop AI systems that are not only technically superior—achieving human-like prosody in speech—but also fundamentally fair and secure in decentralized environments.
